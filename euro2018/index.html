AIMS

1. To consolidate development efforts

2. To set a roadmap for future development priorities

3. To grow the user community, and especially to encourage sideways interaction between users

We will set aside each afternoon of the workshop as a hackfest, to collaboratively implement new features and tests

LOCAL INFORMATION

The workshop will be held in "Aula Polvani", close to the main entrance of the Physics Department in via Celoria 16, 20133, Milano. Below or by clicking here you will find a map of the area.

How to reach the department from Airports:
LINATE: Bus 73 at the airport toward the city centre, then bus n. 93. A taxi ride from the airport to the department costs around 20 €.
BERGAMO-ORIO: Shuttle to the central train station, then metro green line (linea M2) up to the stop Piola, then ten minutes walking. A taxi ride to the department costs around 100 €.
MALPENSA: Shuttle to the central train station, then metro green line (linea M2) up to the stop Piola, then ten minutes walking. A taxi ride to the department costs around 100 €.
